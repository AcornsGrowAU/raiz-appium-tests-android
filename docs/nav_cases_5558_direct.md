## Convergences / aliases / mismatches to DOCUMENT (encoded as assertions above)

1. **3-way Dag/Yodlee picker convergence** (A2, A4, A6 / B2, B4, B6): `rewards_linked_accounts`, `accounts/rewards`, `accounts/financial_insights` all land on the same account-consent picker (rewards variants and the insights variant differ only by intro sentence). `accounts/rewards` is an exact duplicate of `rewards_linked_accounts` (map §6 #3).
2. **`rewards_auto` is an alias** of `raiz_rewards` → Rewards Earn surface, NOT a distinct auto-rewards screen (map §6 #1). A3 asserts the real Earn markers.
3. **`finance` empty-state**: lands on My Finance with the "Set up your financial insights" onboarding card at "0 of 3 completed" — the real destination is the empty/onboarding state (A5).
4. **Empty-state (related, Rewards Track)**: Rewards Track tab shows "There are no rewards at the moment" — covered by `test_rewards.py::TestRewardsTrackValues`; not a direct-nav deep-link area but the second empty-state in this bucket.
5. **Alias pairs (deep link == Settings/drawer route)**: `fees`==Settings "Plans and fees" (A10); `notifications_settings`==Settings "Manage notifications" (A9); `invite_friends`==Settings "Refer a friend" (A13); `finance`==drawer "My Finance" (A5); `offsetters`==drawer "Offsetters" (A11).
6. **Back-stack contrast (deep-link Home vs route-specific landing)**: deep-link entry to fees/financial-insights/offsetters returns to **Home** (B6, B10, B11), whereas the Settings-row equivalents return to **Settings** (RAIZ-9994) and the drawer "Offsetters" returns to the **Drawer** (map §2 quirk). Worth explicit cross-assertion.
